The Venus Versa™ IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) Photofacial Treatment


The Venus Versa IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) photofacial treatment uses filtered, light energy to penetrate the epidermal, mid dermal and hypodermal layer of the skin. Photofacial treatments are meant to tighten, lighten, brighten, rejuvenate and treat skin conditions like acne,

hyperpigmentation (which includes dark spots, age and liver spots, freckles, dyschromia, uneven skin tone, sun spots and pigmented acne scars).It may also offer therapeutic benefits in response to vascular lesions, such as such as rosacea, telangiectasia, spider angioma and ruby points.
